Touching Hearts, Opening Minds

Making Children In Low Income Areas Interested In Having an Education

Welcome to THRIVE, a transformative initiative dedicated to uplifting and empowering the youth of underrepresented communities through education and mentorship. Inspired by a commitment to change, we strive to bridge the educational gap and inspire a new generation of leaders.

ABOUT US

During my time serving underrepresented communities through the Alternative Spring Break program in Howard University, I mananged to interact with the community and something I noticed was how the kids and youth in the community did not feel represented, understood and empowered. This made me wonder if there is any help I can offer to them.

The issue is the lack of underrepresentation, lack of understanding, and disempowerment among children and youth in underserved communities. This problem arises due to systemic factors such as limited access to resources, educational opportunities, and positive role models. When individuals do not see themselves reflected in their communities or feel understood and supported, it can lead to feelings of alienation, low self-esteem, and limited aspirations. This perpetuates a cycle of disadvantage and hampers the development of these communities. Addressing this issue is crucial for fostering inclusivity, empowerment, and social mobility among marginalized youth

OUR STORY

Founded during an Alternative Spring Break program at Howard University, THRIVE began as a small but passionate effort to reach out to local youths feeling overlooked by their community. Recognizing the need for positive role models and guidance, a group of dedicated individuals came together to create a program focused on mentorship and empowerment. What started as a humble initiative has now evolved into a comprehensive mentorship platform, transforming the lives of young people and shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.

MISSION STATEMENT

At THRIVE, our mission is to empower, educate, and elevate young minds by providing them with the tools, resources, and mentorship they need to not only succeed but to excel in their communities and beyond. We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances. Through our comprehensive mentorship platform, we are committed to supporting them on their journey towards success, helping them develop the skills, confidence, and resilience they need to thrive in today's world and become the leaders of tomorrow.

Impact!

Since our inception, we have engaged with over 500 mentees and 200 mentors, forging strong connections and lasting relationships that have had a profound impact on the lives of those involved. We are proud to share that an impressive 90% of our mentees have gone on to pursue higher education, breaking barriers and overcoming obstacles along the way.

We have reached out to young people at various levels, including high schools, making them feel represented and empowered by providing them with relatable role models and mentors. By seeing individuals like themselves succeeding in college and beyond, our mentees have been inspired to dream big and strive for excellence.

Our alumni are making significant contributions in a wide range of fields, from technology to the arts, and we continue to be inspired by their achievements and the positive change they are creating in the world. Their success is a testament to the transformative power of mentorship and the importance of providing support and guidance to young people as they navigate their educational and professional journeys.
